About SafeTrack

SafeTrack is a comprehensive digital platform designed to address the challenges faced by migrant workers and the agencies that support them. Our system bridges the gap between workers, employers, and law enforcement to create a safer, more transparent environment.

Launched in 2023, SafeTrack has already registered over 15,000 workers and partnered with more than 200 employers across the region. Our innovative approach has led to a 32% reduction in migrant-related crimes and improved working conditions for thousands.

Our Mission

To ensure the safety, dignity, and legal employment of all migrant workers through technology-driven solutions and inter-departmental coordination.

Our Vision

To create a nationwide ecosystem where migrant workers can thrive in safe, legal employment with full protection of their rights.
