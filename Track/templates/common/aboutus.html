About SafeTrack

A comprehensive digital platform designed to ensure the safety, security, and well-being of migrant workers through innovative technology and inter-departmental coordination.

Our Mission

To ensure the safety, dignity, and legal employment of all migrant workers through technology-driven solutions and inter-departmental coordination. We strive to create a transparent ecosystem that protects worker rights while streamlining administrative processes.

Our Vision

To create a nationwide ecosystem where migrant workers can thrive in safe, legal employment with full protection of their rights. We envision a future where all migrant workers are registered, tracked, and protected through a unified digital platform.

Our Story

SafeTrack was founded in 2023 by a team of social workers, technologists, and law enforcement professionals who recognized the growing challenges faced by migrant workers. With increasing migration and rising concerns about worker safety and crime rates, there was a clear need for a comprehensive solution.

Our initial pilot program in three districts showed remarkable results - a 32% reduction in migrant-related crimes and improved working conditions for over 5,000 registered workers. Encouraged by this success, we expanded our platform to cover the entire state.

Our Journey

January 2023

Project Inception

SafeTrack concept developed with initial funding from the state government's social welfare department.

June 2023

Pilot Launch

Successfully launched in three districts with 15 police stations and 50 employers participating.

December 2023

Statewide Expansion

Expanded to cover all districts with over 15,000 registered workers and 200+ employers.

March 2024

National Recognition

Awarded "Best Digital Initiative for Migrant Welfare" by the National Council for Labor Welfare.
